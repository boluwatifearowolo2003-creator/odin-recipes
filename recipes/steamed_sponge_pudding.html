Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 cups of granulated sugar
  • 250g butter
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 3 beaten eggs
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tsp mixed spice
  • 1 tablespoons of baking soda.

Steps:

  1. Place flour and sugar in a bowl, rub in butter till it resembles breadcrumbs,
  2. add mixed spices and baking soda.
  3. Make a well in the middle and add beaten eggs and lastly, add sugar.
  4. Put in a greased basin and steam for 2 hours.
