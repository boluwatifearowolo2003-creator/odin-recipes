Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 cups of granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cups of unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup of orange juice
  • 1/4 cup of puppy seeds
  • 2 tablespoons of baking powder
  • Zest of 2 oranges

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 dF
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in almond extract and orange zest.
  4. In another bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and poppy seeds.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients alternatively with milk and orange juice, starting and ending with dry ingredients.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased 9-inch cake pan.
  7. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.
  8. Let cool, then it's ready!
